My Blueberry Nights
Legendary filmmaker Wong Kar-Wai directs Jude Law, Natalie Portman, Rachel Weisz, and Norah Jones in his first English-language feature film -- a romantic road movie detailing the cross-country journey of a woman who sets off across the United States in hopes of mending her broken heart. Elizabeth (Jones) has just been through a particularly nasty break-up, and now she's ready to leave her friends and memories behind as she chases her dreams across the country. In order to support herself on her journey, Elizabeth picks up a series of waitress jobs along the way. As Elizabeth crosses paths with a series of lost souls whose yearnings are even greater than her own - including a troubled cop (David Straithairn), his estranged wife (Rachel Weisz), and an embittered gambler (Natalie Portman) - their emotional turmoil ultimately helps her gain a greater understanding of her own problems. ~ Jason Buchanan, All Movie Guide

这是最近我看的电影，可以说都是比较喜欢的片子，其中有一些是一直想看，又没机会看的。趁着寒假eMule下来欣赏了。
其中印象最深刻的我想是 Basic Instinct 和 My Blueberry Nights 吧。一部片子的视觉很重要。
Basic Instinct 我越看越感觉压力很大，而导演成功地给我们和戏中主人公完全不同的视觉和感官世界。每一次冰锥出现的镜头都是那么完美。可以说是一部细节很丰富的电影。
My Blueberry Nights 相比之下起来轻松很多，然而虽然片中对人和对感情的探索我很多没有看懂。然而片子不直接特写爱情而专注生活的雕琢让我感觉电影很美，很多时刻被刻画得很诱惑。
