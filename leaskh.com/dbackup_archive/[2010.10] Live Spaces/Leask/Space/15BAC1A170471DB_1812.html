iPhoto 6 update(6.0.4)

6/21/2006 4:07:11 PM


This iPhoto update contains a variety of new greeting card and postcard themes for use with Apple print services, including invite and thank you card designs for summer parties, weddings, birthdays, and more.

虽然我大多时候已经用Aperture管理照片了，但是偶尔会用iPhoto管理一下图片素材。这个版本的iPhoto主要加强Card和Postcard的Themes。其实iPhoto生成Card和Postcard等印刷品的向导挺好用的，成品都很精美。只可惜需要Apple print services，不能生成PDF用于本地印刷。这一点比较遗憾。

Get the Update:
http://www.apple.com/support/downloads/iphoto604.html
